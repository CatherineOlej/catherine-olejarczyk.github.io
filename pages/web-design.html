About Rate My Learning

Who We Are

We are graduated students of Conestoga College from the IT innovation and Design 3-year program. We each have specific expertise in the web development and design industry, and have showcased our skills in this web application.

Our Mission

Rate My Learning is a review-based website designed to assist students in aiding their decision for their future career path program direction.

Rate My Learning is an easy-to-use web application that gives you the power to:

  • Voice your opinion – Create reviews and inform others about a particular program or course that you participated in.
  • Manage your reviews– Made a mistake or don’t want a review posted anymore? Easily edit and delete reviews you have created at the touch of a fingertip.
  • See what others are saying– Easily browse through reviews and see what other students or faculty members are saying about a particular program or course.
  • Protect yourself – Have the ability to remain anonymous if you do not feel comfortable displaying your name on the web.

Our Products and Services

Rate My Learning is a web application that will allow users, mainly students and/or faculty to anonymously submit feedback about their personal experiences at a post-secondary school. Users will be able to create, edit, delete and browse reviews made by others and themselves. The intended audience for this application is current students or anyone looking to enroll into a post-secondary college institution. Accessibility to the site will be via desktop or mobile devices in order to allow users full functionality and on demand services

Conclusion

With the introduction of Rate My Learning, not only will students be informed on what they are choosing but schools can also use this data to better their services and create an overall better learning environment for students across Canada.
